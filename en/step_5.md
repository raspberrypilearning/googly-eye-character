## Backdrop effects

You can also make the project do something when you click on the Stage.

--- task ---

--- collapse ---

---
title: Change backdrop effects when Stage clicked
---

```blocks3
when stage clicked
change [color v] effect by [25]
```

--- /collapse ---


--- collapse ---

---
title: Switch backdrop when Stage clicked
---

```blocks3
when stage clicked
next backdrop
```

--- /collapse ---
